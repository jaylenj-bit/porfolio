This was the result of my first ever congessional app challenge. I loved this game so much because I taught myself how to use sprite lab and months after still edited the game.

This was my second game made for the congressional app challenge. The game is called Magic Mage, It's a fun simple game to kill time while keeping you entertained and testing your reflexes. It's a game where you play as a wizard who was scrolling through the desert one day and is suddenly being attacked by monsters. You use your magic but they just keep coming. After every 5 you get rid of there's a chance that they use a powerful spell on you that you have to dodge out of the way from. I like this game a lot because it helped me learn more about sprites and I still want to edit it to have more features.
